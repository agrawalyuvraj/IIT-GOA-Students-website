ABOUT US

Greetings from IIT Goa's Culturals! At IIT Goa we undertake a variety of cultural activities, which are broadly classified under 6 genres – Dance, Music, Dramatics, Fine Arts, Literary, and Photography.

Culturals @IIT Goa promotes a vibrant and electic campus experience through organizing innumerable multifarious activities pertaining to the cultural events, bringing people from all walks, interests, and ethnicity come together in ways to stimulate community interaction.

All starts from the freshers party ‘Fresco Fiesta’ welcoming the new entrants to the new family, to Independence Day and Republic Day, from Eid to Diwali, Christmas to Lohri, Teacher’s Day to Children’s Day, and the most exciting ‘CultRang’. IIT Goa’s cultural council, with a blend of Goan culture presents extravaganza of art and culture, entertaining and enriching the lives of the people at the institute.

Originals

Description

In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality.” Originals, the photography club of IIT Goa, being one of the most active clubs go IIT Goa, encourages amateur photographers and beginners interested in learning the techniques of photography by providing them with all the latest equipment ranging from professional cameras, tripods to camcorders, etc.

Kalam

Description

Every secret of a writer's soul, every experience of his life, every quality of his mind, is written large in his works.” Qalam, the literary club of IIT Goa, aims at developing and honing the literary skills of the students. The club inspires students to develop a taste for literature and also works in the direction of expanding their horizon of spoken and written language. The club is based upon using the creativity skills it takes to be a writer.

Club Head

Meraki

Description

To dance is to be out of yourself. Larger, more beautiful, more powerful… This is power, it is glory on earth and it is yours for the taking” Meraki, the dance club of IIT Goa, is one of the most glamorous and hardworking clubs. Due to the overwhelming participation shown by dedicated students, various workshops and events are conducted throughout the year and the performances are much awaited by everyone.

Mukhota

Description

All the world's a stage and most of us are desperately unrehearsed. “ Mukhota, Dramatics club of IIT Goa, is one of the oldest and most talented clubs. From writing plays, to direction and to perform, the members get to learn each of the skills mentioned and excel in them. The club is home to all those who are passionate towards theatre and streetplays.

Orion

Description

Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything.” Orion, the music club of IIT Goa, is a place where students come to jam, learn and make music of different kinds. It seeks to bring together everyone with a passion for any genre of music. Being one of the most active clubs, the club conducts productive music workshops and gives fierce competition to other artists.

Panache

Description

The object of oratory alone in not truth, but persuasion.” Panache, the oratory club of IIT Goa, aims at enhancing the student’s ability to communicate their feelings by understanding their own emotions. The club provides a platform to the orators to converge and hone their debating, leadership and public speaking skills.

Description

The work of art is a scream of freedom.”  Eunoia, the Fine Arts club of IIT Goa, is one of the most hardworking clubs and shows active participation from students. Through various distinctive and immersive events and workshops, a vibrant and colourful atmosphere is created all over the campus. This is the right place for any art lover or enthusiast!
